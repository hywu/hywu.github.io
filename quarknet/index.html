QuarkNet is a nationwide collaboration partnering high-school teachers and high-energy physicists. Here at SMU we are exicted to continue offering an annual workshop to teachers in the Dallas-Fort Worth Metroplex

The workshop includes morning lectures from SMU/UTSW faculty members on the latest developments in high-energy physics, cosmology, astrophysics, medical physics, and science education.
